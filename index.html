Amphora Typology Database

Amphora Identification with 3D Model Matching

The Amphora Typology Database is designed to support archaeologists, researchers, museum professionals, underwater survey teams, and enthusiasts who need a fast and reliable way to identify ancient amphoras.


Use the Catalogue page to browse hundreds of amphora types organized by shape and typology, rim and shoulder form, handle profile and section, neck, body, and base shape, chronology and production region, fabric and dimensions.


Upload a photo of an amphora, and AmphorAI will match it to the closest known model in our dataset using machine learning and computer vision.
