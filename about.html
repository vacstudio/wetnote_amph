About WetNote Amphora Catalogue

WetNote is a growing digital platform designed to support divers, archaeologists, researchers, and conservation teams working in the aquatic world. The project begins with a specialized focus: an advanced Amphora Typology Catalogue built to help users explore, compare, and understand ancient amphoras using structured data, drawings, images, and 3D models.

Why This Catalogue?

Amphoras are key to understanding ancient trade, production, and maritime activity. However, identifying them is often slow and requires years of typological familiarity. This catalogue aims to simplify that process by providing:

  • ‚úî A structured typology browser with detailed filters
  • ‚úî Drawings, photographs, and SVG silhouettes
  • ‚úî Interactive 3D models for visual comparison
  • ‚úî Metadata and measurements linked to archaeological sources

Beyond Amphoras: A Platform for Aquatic Projects

Although our journey begins with amphora typology, WetNote is designed to grow into a wider project-management ecosystem for underwater work. In the future, the platform will support tools for:

What Is AmphorAI?

AmphorAI is an upcoming feature that will use image analysis and machine learning to help users quickly identify amphoras from photos. It will compare the uploaded image against the models in our dataset and return the closest typological matches. AmphorAI is still in development but represents the future direction of the platform‚Äîcombining traditional archaeology with modern computational tools.
