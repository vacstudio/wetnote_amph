Join Our Volunteer Team

WetNote is seeking passionate volunteers to contribute to our **Amphora Typology Database**. If you are interested in archaeology, ceramics, or data curation, this is a great opportunity to participate in an international, open-access project.

Who We're Looking For

  • Archaeology students or enthusiasts with knowledge of ancient ceramics
  • Researchers or volunteers interested in typology and cataloguing
  • People with experience in data entry, photography, or 3D modeling
  • Anyone passionate about preserving and sharing historical data

How You Can Contribute

  1. Help with morphological descriptions of amphoras (rim, neck, body, base, handles)
  2. Compare and verify forms against literature and existing typologies
  3. Assist in creating catalogue entries including images, drawings, and 3D models
  4. Support data cleaning and validation for the JSON database
  5. Provide feedback or suggestions for improving the catalogue interface

Get Involved

If you are interested in contributing, please contact us with your background and area of interest. We welcome volunteers from all around the world.

Note: This is a voluntary position. Your contributions will be acknowledged in the database and related publications.
